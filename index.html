Randy Ramos Salon

About Us

Randy Ramos Salon has been providing its services to Skokie starting in 2007. Since then, Randy has been awarded the Skokie Merchant of the Year Award in 2009 and 2014. Randy has been a hair stylist for over 25 years and specializes in haircuts, highlights, and color for women, men, and children. Scheduling is done by appointment only. To book an appointment please call or leave a message at (847) 675-5587.
